Szekler Pictures - How we see it

We don’t wait for the perfect moment or a giant budget - we simply make films. Our short films are centered around experimentation, learning and the joy of storytelling. Szekler Pictures is a creative lab, where amateur excitement meets a professional vision. We didn’t set out to make world-changing epics, simply to capture moments worth experiencing for 3-5 minutes.
