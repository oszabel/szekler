Our Mission: Every minute matters

We believe that the most impactful stories don’t need a two-hour runtime and millions of dollars. At Szekler Pictures we capture everything that counts between 2 to 7 minutes: honest moments, unexpected turns, and raw emotions. We don’t make films, because we have all the best technical equipment, but because we have something to say. Our goal is to utilize the freedom of independent filmmaking, and open windows anyone would be happy to look through for the length of a coffee break.

The Szekler Method: Learning by creating

Szekler Pictures isn’t a static studio, rather a living experiment. Our method is centered around lifelong learning: every film is a lesson. We aren’t afraid of mistakes, because every blurred frame or bump along the road brings us closer to the next level. For us, the word ‘amateur’ carries its original meaning: someone who does it out of love. We approach the camera with this passion every single day.
